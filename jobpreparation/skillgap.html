About This Skill

Communication is the ability to convey or share ideas and feelings effectively. It's one of the most important soft skills in the workplace, enabling better collaboration, problem-solving, and relationship building.

Improvement Tips

  • Practice active listening in conversations
  • Work on your body language and eye contact
  • Record yourself speaking to identify areas for improvement
  • Join a public speaking group like Toastmasters
  • Read books on effective communication
